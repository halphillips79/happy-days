About The Happy Days Character Appearance Database

What is The Happy Days Character Appearance Database?

The Happy Days Character Appearance Database is a database of which Happy Days characters have appeared in scenes together. You can use this database to look up which characters have appeared together the most often, who's appeared with a given character the most often, etc.

What does or doesn't count as appearing together?

Any time two characters appear in a scene together, that counts as a "co-appearance", even if they don't interact. This doesn't include fantasy sequences, dream sequences, etc., because that's not really the character, that's just Richie's subconscious mind pretending to be Fonzie.

Why are the results incomplete?

Because this site is a work in progress! It will be complete as soon as I've finished watching every episode of Happy Days.
